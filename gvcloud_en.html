The Question Everyone Asks
"What happens
if the internet
goes down?"

The doors keep working. All credentials and access permissions are stored locally on the controller. If the connection drops, the system runs normally. Entries are logged locally. Once connectivity is restored, everything syncs back to the cloud automatically. Nothing is lost.

Cloud is the management layer, not the engine. The engine is in the controller, and it runs regardless of network status. Once you explain it that way, most customers are satisfied.
